Woven Dreams

Walking through New York City, I feel a mix of different emotions. Sometimes, it's like a comforting dream with soft colors like purple and pink, especially when I look at the city lights. But when I'm in busy places like Times Square or downtown Manhattan, it gets kind of crazy with bright yellow and energetic blue all around, matching the city's fast pace. Central Park is like a calm, green oasis in the middle of it all. New York can also feel a bit tough and challenging, like when everything turns a bit gray. Yet, there's a special kind of purity in places like the Brooklyn Bridge with its bright white lights. Overall, New York, with its changing colors of purple, pink, yellow, blue, green, gray, and white, makes me feel both amazed and thoughtful.
